# What games can I play?

<!-- TODO: are there cases where it doesn't? -->
The Steam {term}`snap` aims to support the same games as the Steam deb.

## Resources on compatibility

[{term}`Steam Deck` verified games](https://store.steampowered.com/greatondeck) are
verified by Valve to work on the {term}`Steam Deck` (Linux-based) and are generally expected
to work on desktop Linux distributions.

{term}`ProtonDB` is a great resource for finding community information about specific games and their playability.

## Filtering your game library

To filter your own library by *Steam Deck Verification*,
go to your Steam {guilabel}`Library` tab and click the penguin
([Tux](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tux_(mascot))) to show games that run on
Linux. 

Alternatively, click the {guilabel}`Advanced filtering options` button, expand the
drop-down under {guilabel}`Hardware Support`, and choose a level of verification to filter
by.
